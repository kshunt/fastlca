Life cycle boundary — hover each stage
PRODUCT STAGE (A1–A3)
A1Raw materials
Extraction and processing of raw materials. Usually the largest share of embodied carbon for most construction products.
A2Transport
Transport of raw materials to the manufacturing plant.
A3Manufacturing
Energy and process emissions at the factory where the product is made.
CONSTRUCTION (A4–A5)
A4To site
Transport from factory gate to construction site. Set distance and vehicle type in the A4–A5 tab.
A5On site
Site energy use and material waste during installation.
USE PHASE — NOT INCLUDED
B1–5Use & maintenance
Not included. Requires project-specific service life data.
B6–7Energy & water
Operational energy and water use. Not included in this version.
END OF LIFE (C1–C4)
C1Demolition
Energy for deconstruction at end of life. Typically small relative to A1–A3.
C2Transport
Waste transport to processing or disposal facility.
C3Processing
Sorting, shredding and reprocessing of waste material.
C4Disposal
Final landfill or incineration without energy recovery.
BEYOND
DNet benefit
Recycling / reuse credit beyond the system boundary. Reported separately — never added to A1–C4 total per EN 15804+A2:2019.
