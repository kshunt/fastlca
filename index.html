Methodology & data sources — how FastLCA works ▼
FastLCA calculates Global Warming Potential (GWP) in kg CO₂e, following the module structure of EN 15804+A2:2019 and the general methodology of ISO 14040/14044. Results are indicative estimates — not a certified LCA or EPD.
A1–A3 — Generic materials
ICE Database v3.0 (University of Bath, 2019). Widely used, peer-reviewed generic values representing UK/European industry averages. Open database ↗
A1–A3 — EPD products
Product-specific values entered from verified manufacturer EPDs. FastLCA does not pre-load values to avoid outdated data — users verify against the current EPD document.
A4 — Transport to site
ECTA (2011) transport emission factors per vehicle type and distance (kg CO₂e per tonne·km). User-specified.
A5 — Construction process
IEA 2023 grid intensities (user-selectable) + waste factor (%) applied to A1–A3, representing material off-cuts and over-ordering.
C1–C4 — End of life
Generic per-kg factors estimated from published EPD ranges by material category and EOL scenario. For EPD products with declared C1–C4, those are used directly.
Module D — Beyond boundary
Net benefit from material recovery. Always reported separately — never added to A1–C4 total per EN 15804+A2:2019 clause 6.4.3.4.
⚠ Limitations: ICE v3.0 values are UK/European averages from 2019. Generic C1–C4 and Module D values are estimated — not from a single verified source. For project-specific accuracy, use product EPDs. FastLCA is not a substitute for a certified LCA or EPD.

USE PHASE — NOT INCLUDED
B1–5Use & maint.
Not included. Requires project-specific service life data.
B6–7Energy/water
Operational energy and water. Not included.
END OF LIFE (C1–C4)
C1Demolition
Energy for deconstruction at end of life.
C2Transport
Waste transport to processing or disposal facility.
C3Processing
Sorting, shredding and reprocessing of waste material.
C4Disposal
Final landfill or incineration without energy recovery.
BEYOND
DNet benefit
Recycling/reuse credit. Reported separately — NEVER added to A1–C4 total per EN 15804+A2:2019.
